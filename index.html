Welcome To Raheja Jade City

A new project by the well-known K Raheja Corp, created to bring fresh, modern living to Juinagar, Navi Mumbai. K Raheja Jade City offers large luxurious 1, 2, 2.5 & 3 BHK homes that combine style with comfort. Residents can enjoy a premium lifestyle with top amenities like a lounge, swimming pool, gym, landscaped gardens, and fun activity zones for all age groups.

  • 70% open spaces embraced by the lush Parsik Hills
